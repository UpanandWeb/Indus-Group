  • detailed design documentation
  • tender documentation

  • co-ordination of sub consultants
  • tender management
  • contract administration

“Our focus is to create spaces that enhance our clients’ business.”

Sanwoods Interiors recognise that their success depends on understanding what makes a company unique. Working in close collaboration with their clients, the long-term corporate goals of each individual client are considered in providing flexible and functional solutions.
