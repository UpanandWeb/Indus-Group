Our Services :
At ACE INDUS, we understand the value of "Right time, Right skills" toward enhancement of productivity and gaining competitive edge. Our ability to understand the client's requirement and the speed at which we deliver results with high ethical standards have contributed to building our reputation in the industry.

We offer recruitment solutions to national and international organisations. With a huge databank of more than 2,00,000 candidates, we deliver you the best talent in the industry.

Facilities provided to candidates/clients:

  • Online registration from across the world, at no cost to the candidate
  • Open positions of various technologies for clients with diverse experience bands
  • Screening of candidates by phone and mail
  • Presenting excellent quality resumes to the reputed clients
  • Short listing of candidates after successful clearing of various rounds of interviews
  • Providing airfare at company’s cost to outstation candidates attending interviews
  • Pick and drop facility made available for candidates from other cities

Expertise:
ACE INDUS has been in the field of HR for about eight years. It has collaborated with several leading companies. We provide our clients the latest and in-depth information on market trends. Our long presence in the Indian market gives us a cutting edge in any IT consulting assignment.

Strengths:
ACE INDUS offers it clients actionable business recommendations based on current industry trends. Our strengths are our vast knowledgebase, wide coverage and vast industry network. Our business experience and skilled human resource pool ensure insightful and immediate solutions.

Recruitment & Placement:
ACE INDUS facilitates recruitment/placement to various organizations of national and international repute. This is done with the help of specialized knowledge and skills, honed over years of partnership with some of the most renowned companies. Armed with a strong database of more than two lakh profiles, a strong network of professionals and a clear understanding of the job-market, ACE INDUS has confirmed its ability to find the perfect fit that caters to the demands of the various companies.

We strive to differ by:
  • Understanding the client's requirements and not just interpreting them
  • Providing the best, both from the employer's as well as the employee's perspective
  • Ensuring least attrition and high productivity
  • For the convenience of our clients and candidates, ACE INDUS will soon extend its operations to different parts of India and the world.
