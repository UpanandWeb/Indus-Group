Website Maintenance :
Creating a website is easier than maintaining it. Over the years that we have been developing websites we have discovered that maintaining a site can require more money than developing it.

If you don't want to spend your time updating your website, then let us do it for you so that you can focus on what you do best. We make Website Maintenance a simple, stress-free experience. You will be assigned a single contact person who knows you and your project - you won't be shuffled around and have to start at square, the one explaining your project and needs. We are specialized in providing personalized custom service - it's the reason so many of our clients have been with us for longer period.

Our quality control process ensures that your Website Maintenance job gets detailed attention and accuracy that is essential for your site's success. Communication is easily handled with e-mail, fax and telephone. We always try to accommodate any rush or emergency changes from our clients for example, we can change content of website within a short notice period of one working day.

Website maintenance has become an important activity of the business as websites have become the vital part of business and sales lead generators. Website maintenance has become a must these days since even if the servers are good offering 99% uptime, still its important to check the forms and mail links at least weekly once to be double sure that the enquiries are being forwarded from the website perfectly.

We offer you cost-effective website maintenance and take on the task of routine website maintenance to existing web sites and make whatever changes are needed as and when required. By outsourcing your web site maintenance, you are free from the regular updates and expansions.

Our regular site maintenance includes :

Checking and Updating links to keep the site away from broken links.

Verifying the site is up and running, and getting the site back up if it's down.

Minor word/Sentence Changes/Corrections.

Checking search engines for ranking and making minor SEO adjustments as needed.

Adding/Changing/Deleting email addresses.

Answering client questions about the site, search engines, browsers, etc. correcting broken scripts.

Keeping an eye on statistics.

Maintenance regarding security issues (scripts, databases, image/bandwidth theft, etc.)

Log file analysis and visitor/site statistics reports, creating FAQs and searchable knowledge bases.

Retaining a full backup copy of the web site, Communication with third-party service providers (credit card processor, hosting company,information suppliers) on your behalf.
