Our Verticals :
Technology
The technology segment in the Indian industrial scenario has witnessed a tremendous upsurge and expansion and the trend continues. Hiring is at its frenzied peak while the numbers to hire dwindle. There is a more than obvious resource crunch.

Ace Indus, with its capabilities of handling complex requirements across multiple technology domains, across products, consulting and services, is ideally positioned to provide quality alternatives, its focus reinforced with its cross-industry exposure.

With the IT industry slated to grow in the next three years to a whopping US$ 60-70 billion the field is wide open for players to prove themselves in the IT and ITES, SW and Testing segments.

Consumer, Retail & Telecom
The FMCG market segment has been making unprecedented inroads into markets ,both urban and rural with a plethora of innovative and appealing products.

The FMCG is a more of a people-oriented industry than anything else, on both sides of the sales counter. Operational excellence is the key with people driving it. The need for highly innovative and original professionals has never been greater in the face of expanding logistics and complex supply chain requirements.

With increased FDI and the rise of retail/consumer goods segment the service dimension has come into the spotlight. As per estimates, Retail sales in India's consumer goods market are expected to grow to US$ 400 billion by 2010.

Driving growth in the telecom sector is the mobile telephone revolution, with all the major cell phone manufacturers like Nokia, Sony Ericsson, LG, Samsung, Haier and Motorola having now set up bases in India.

Ace Indus, with its acknowledged track record and growing presence has been a dependable source for quality resources in Consumer, Retail and Telecom sectors
