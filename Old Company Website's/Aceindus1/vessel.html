VESSEL & CARGO CHARTERING

SSPL chartering / brokerage and consultancy service offers its clientele 24 / 7 exclusive service on competitive basis.

SSPL chartering is broadly segmented into Dry cargo division which arrange fixtures of dry cargo and vessels of all sizes, world-wide. Time charters or Spot voyage charters along C.O.A.s wuld constitute a matter of choice at any given time and the individual needs of our clientele would be met through our successful negotiating power built over the years.

SSPL actively involved in all areas especially East Coast India, South East Asia and Andaman & Nicobar Islands. We consider ourselves strong enough in handling steel cargo, import of Timber Logs from Myanmar and Malaysia and prominent in Andaman & Nicobar, Male sectors.
